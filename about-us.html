OUR HISTORY

A Legacy Trusted by Generations

A Legacy Trusted by Generations

In 2006, Amol Foods began its journey in Chapi village, a quaint town in Gujarat’s Banaskantha district, with the production of Chakki Fresh Atta. From these humble beginnings, we quickly expanded, supplying Chakki Fresh Atta to renowned restaurants and hotels across Gujarat and Mumbai. By establishing a dedicated office and godown in Jogeshwari, Mumbai, we solidified our presence in the bustling city.

Driven by innovation and customer demand, we diversified our offerings to include Tandoori Atta, R. Atta, semolina, besan, and maida, catering to the unique needs of HORECA (Hotels, Restaurants, and Catering) clients. Over time, our product portfolio grew to over 500 items, providing everything under one roof.

Our journey of expansion continued with the launch of premium-quality rice in 2010, followed by the introduction of Amol Spices in 2021 and cashew nuts in 2022. Guided by a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we remain a trusted partner for households and businesses alike, delivering excellence with every product.
